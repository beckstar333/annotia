Frequently asked questions
Can I cancel Pro anytime?▾
Yes. Cancel anytime from your dashboard. You'll keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.
What happens when I hit 5 AI summaries on Free?▾
You can still browse papers, read existing summaries, and participate in discussions. You just can't generate new AI summaries until the next month — or you can upgrade to Pro for unlimited.
Is Annotia only for academics?▾
Not at all. Annotia is for anyone who wants to engage with research — clinicians, students, journalists, curious minds. The AI summaries are specifically designed to make papers accessible to non-specialists.
What papers can I access?▾
We index over 250 million open access papers from OpenAlex and Semantic Scholar. We only surface freely available research — no paywalled content, ever.
Will there be a team/institutional plan?▾
We're exploring this. If your institution is interested, reach out and we'll prioritise it.
